Home Management

Practical, dependable support that helps your home remain organised, prepared and running smoothly.

View full details

Home Management is designed for clients who want the reassurance of knowing that important household tasks are being handled. Support can be used regularly or arranged around particularly busy periods, holidays, family visits or changing circumstances.

What can be included

  • Changing bedding and preparing bedrooms
  • Laundry and ironing arrangements
  • Receiving household deliveries
  • Waiting in for tradespeople
  • Coordinating repair appointments
  • Restocking household essentials
  • Putting bins out and bringing them in
  • Watering indoor and outdoor plants
  • Preparing your home for visitors
  • Seasonal household tasks
  • General home checks
  • Light practical household assistance

Ideal for

  • Busy professionals
  • Families with demanding routines
  • Older adults living independently
  • People recovering from illness
  • Second-home owners
  • Clients preparing for guests or events

Typical examples

You might ask Sorted. to meet a plumber while you are at work, change and prepare bedding before family arrive, receive a furniture delivery, restock essential household items or check that heating and appliances are working properly.

How it works

Before support begins, we agree your priorities and any instructions relating to your home. Tasks can then be completed during scheduled visits, with clear updates provided where required.

Why clients value Home Management

Small household tasks often create a surprising amount of pressure when they begin to build up. Home Management gives you one dependable point of contact who understands your preferences and quietly ensures that the details are taken care of.

Organisation

Home Organisation

Calm, practical organisation that makes your home easier to use, maintain and enjoy.

View full details

Home Organisation is not about creating an unrealistic show home. It is about developing sensible systems that work for the way you live, making belongings easier to find and everyday routines simpler to manage.

Areas we can organise

  • Kitchens and food storage
  • Wardrobes and clothing
  • Bedrooms
  • Utility rooms
  • Cupboards and storage spaces
  • Garages and sheds
  • Home offices
  • Children’s belongings
  • Paperwork and household documents
  • Seasonal decorations
  • Moving and unpacking
  • Digital files and photographs

What support can include

  • Sorting items into clear categories
  • Decluttering at your pace
  • Creating simple storage systems
  • Labelling cupboards and containers
  • Preparing donations
  • Arranging recycling or disposal
  • Organising items by frequency of use
  • Planning suitable storage purchases

Typical examples

You may want help organising a wardrobe, sorting a kitchen after moving, creating a filing system, preparing belongings for donation or bringing order to a room that has gradually become difficult to use.

A respectful approach

Nothing is removed or discarded without your agreement. Organisation is completed calmly and without judgement, with your comfort, preferences and decision-making at the centre of the process.

Why clients value Home Organisation

A well-organised home can reduce daily frustration and make routines feel significantly easier. The aim is not simply to make a room look better, but to create a system that remains practical after the session ends.

Administrative Support

Professional help with household paperwork, appointments, online tasks and everyday organisation.

View full details

Administrative Support helps you stay on top of the paperwork, appointments and digital tasks involved in managing everyday life. It is practical assistance, not legal, financial or regulated professional advice.

What can be included

  • Sorting household post
  • Creating simple filing systems
  • Scanning and printing documents
  • Completing straightforward forms
  • Booking appointments
  • Calendar organisation
  • Email assistance
  • Online account support
  • Renewal reminders
  • Contacting service providers
  • Preparing documents for appointments
  • Basic spreadsheet organisation

Common areas of support

  • Utility accounts
  • Insurance administration
  • Council correspondence
  • Household subscriptions
  • Appointment letters
  • Contractor paperwork
  • Personal document storage
  • Everyday technology assistance

Typical examples

Sorted. could organise a pile of household post, help prepare documents for an appointment, book a routine service, scan important paperwork, set reminders for renewals or help you navigate a straightforward online account.

Service Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Clear answers to some of the most common questions about using Sorted. services.

Can I combine more than one service?
Yes. Many clients use a combination of services. For example, a visit could include errands, paperwork and household organisation. Your support can be planned around the outcome you need rather than being restricted to one category.
Do I need to book regular support?
No. Services can be booked as one-off or occasional support. Clients who want regular scheduled help can also explore the Sorted. membership options.
How long is a typical visit?
Visit length depends on the work being completed. The expected time will be discussed before the booking so that the task, priorities and likely cost are clear.
Can my support change over time?
Yes. Support can be adjusted as your routine, circumstances or priorities change. You are not expected to keep the same list of tasks indefinitely.
Can you help a relative who lives independently?
Yes, with the relative’s agreement. Sorted. can provide practical household assistance, errands, administrative help and companionship. We do not provide personal care, medical care or medication administration.
Can you accompany me to an appointment?
Accompanied appointments may be available by prior agreement. The arrangement, transport, timings and type of support needed should be discussed when booking.
Do you bring cleaning or specialist equipment?
Sorted. is not a specialist cleaning or trades service. Where ordinary household products or equipment are needed, arrangements will be agreed beforehand. Specialist work should be completed by a suitably qualified provider.
What happens during the complimentary consultation?
The consultation is a relaxed conversation about what you need, how often you may need support and any important preferences or practical considerations. There is no obligation to proceed.
