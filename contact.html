FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Before you get in touch.

A few helpful answers about consultations, bookings and how Sorted. works.

Is the consultation free?

Yes. Your initial consultation is complimentary and there is no obligation to book a service or membership afterwards.

Do I need to know exactly which service I need?

No. You can simply explain what you would like help with. We will then recommend the most suitable service, session or membership.

Can a family member enquire on someone else's behalf?

Yes. Family members can contact us to discuss possible support for a relative. We will always involve the person receiving the service and respect their wishes, privacy and consent.

Do you provide personal or medical care?

No. Sorted. provides practical home and lifestyle support, companionship and organisation. We do not provide personal care, nursing care or medical treatment.

How quickly will you respond?

We aim to respond within one working day, often much sooner. Enquiries received during evenings or weekends will be reviewed on the next working day.

Is one-off support available?

Yes. You can book flexible one-off or ad-hoc support without joining a monthly membership.
