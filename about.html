WHAT TO EXPECT

What a Sorted. visit looks like.

Every visit is calm, organised and shaped around what you need. You will always know what has been agreed, what will happen during the visit and what has been completed before we leave.

1

Before your visit

We confirm the date, arrival time and agreed tasks in advance. Any access arrangements, shopping lists, appointments or special instructions are recorded so everything is clear before we arrive.

2

A calm and punctual arrival

We arrive at the agreed time, introduce ourselves clearly and take a moment to confirm your priorities. There is no rushing and no confusion about what you would like us to handle.

3

Your agreed tasks are completed

We work through the agreed tasks carefully and efficiently, whether that involves errands, organisation, administration, property support, companionship or several smaller jobs during the same visit.

4

Clear communication throughout

Where a decision, purchase or change is needed, we check with you rather than making assumptions. Your preferences, privacy and control remain central throughout every visit.

5

Everything is checked before we leave

We make sure the agreed work is complete, your home is left tidy and any keys, receipts, paperwork or purchases are returned to the correct place.

6

A clear update afterwards

You receive a simple summary of what was completed, along with any useful updates, receipts or matters requiring your attention. For regular clients, future priorities can also be noted for the next visit.

Every visit is personally tailored.

Some clients prefer to be at home while we help. Others arrange secure access and receive an update afterwards. Your visits will always be organised around your comfort, routine and preferences.
