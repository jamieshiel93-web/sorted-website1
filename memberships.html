HOW MEMBERSHIPS WORK

Simple, personal and completely tailored.

Every Sorted. membership begins with a complimentary consultation and is built around you, your home and the way you live. There are no standard packages or one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, we create an ongoing support plan that evolves with your needs.

1. Complimentary Consultation

Everything begins with a relaxed conversation. We'll take the time to understand your home, your routine, your priorities and the areas where regular support would make the biggest difference. This can take place in your home, by telephone or via video call.

2. Personal Recommendation

Following your consultation we'll recommend the most suitable membership based on the amount of support you need, how often you'd like visits and the type of assistance you're looking for. Because every client is different, recommendations are always personal.

3. Your Support Plan

Together we'll agree exactly what you'd like us to look after. That may include shopping, home organisation, household administration, appointments, contractor management, preparing your home before you travel or anything else within our services.

4. Regular Scheduled Visits

Once everything is in place we'll begin your regular visits. Because we become familiar with your home and your preferences, each visit becomes more efficient, more personalised and far more valuable over time.

5. Ongoing Reviews

Life changes and so do your priorities. Your membership can easily be adjusted as your circumstances change. Whether you need more support, less support or something completely different, we'll adapt with you.

6. One Trusted Point of Contact

Instead of juggling multiple companies and appointments, you'll have one trusted local business coordinating everything. It's a simpler, calmer way to stay on top of everyday life.
